# Password Reset & Change Password - User Guide

## How to Reset Your Password

### Step 1: Go to Login Page
1. Open the MWENDO MOJA system
2. You should see the login page

### Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"
1. On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link
2. You'll be taken to the password reset page

### Step 3: Enter Your Email
1. Enter the email address associated with your account
2. Click "Send Reset Link"
3. You'll see a confirmation message

### Step 4: Check Your Email
1. Check your email for a password reset link
2. The link will be valid for 1 hour
3. Click the link to proceed

### Step 5: Create New Password
1. Enter your new password
2. Confirm your password
3. Click "Reset Password"
4. You'll be redirected to login

### Step 6: Login with New Password
1. Use your email and new password to login
2. You're now logged in!

## Password Requirements

Your password MUST contain:
- ✅ At least 8 characters
- ✅ At least one UPPERCASE letter (A-Z)
- ✅ At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- ✅ At least one number (0-9)
- ✅ At least one special character (!@#$%^&*()_+-=[]{};\':"|,.<>/?

### Examples of Valid Passwords:
- `SecurePass@123`
- `MyPassword#456`
- `Welcome2024!`
- `Admin@2024`

### Examples of Invalid Passwords:
- `password` ❌ (no uppercase, number, or symbol)
- `Pass123` ❌ (no special character)
- `PASS@123` ❌ (no lowercase)
- `Pass@1` ❌ (less than 8 characters)

## How to Change Your Password (When Logged In)

### Step 1: Go to Change Password Page
1. Click on your profile menu
2. Select "Change Password"
3. Or navigate to `/change-password`

### Step 2: Enter Current Password
1. Enter your current password
2. This verifies it's really you

### Step 3: Enter New Password
1. Enter your new password
2. Make sure it meets all requirements
3. Confirm your new password

### Step 4: Submit
1. Click "Change Password"
2. You'll see a success message
3. You're still logged in with your new password

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid or expired reset token"
- The reset link has expired (valid for 1 hour)
- Request a new reset link

### "Password does not meet requirements"
- Your password is too weak
- Make sure it has uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol
- Must be at least 8 characters

### "Current password is incorrect"
- You entered the wrong current password
- Try again carefully

### "User not found"
- The email doesn't exist in the system
- Check your email spelling
- Contact your administrator

## Security Tips

1. **Never share your password** with anyone
2. **Use a strong password** that's hard to guess
3. **Change your password regularly** (every 3 months)
4. **Don't use the same password** for multiple accounts
5. **Log out** when using shared computers

## Need Help?

Contact your system administrator if you:
- Can't receive reset emails
- Have other login issues
- Need to reset another user's password
